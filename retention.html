Return

You can return or exchange the product in case of a clear manufacturing defect within 14 (fourteen) days from the date of its receipt. It is possible to return the goods only if its presentation, consumer properties, as well as a document confirming the fact and conditions of purchase of the specified goods (email or sms confirmation) are preserved. During this period, the Buyer is obliged to notify the store manager by writing an email to Lera.Kashina.2018@mail.ru about the desire to return the goods.

RETURN TERMS

The item has not been used

The presentation and consumer properties are preserved: there are no signs of wear on the jewelry, undamaged packaging

No more than 14 (fourteen) days have passed since the receipt of the goods for returns at will and 20 (twenty) days due to manufacturing defects.

According to Decree No. 55, jewelry made of precious metals (silver, gold) cannot be returned.By law, we will not be able to take back jewelry that was made for you to order or modified - changed the size, color of the coating, engraving, etc.

It is possible to return stationery and household goods in case of inadequate quality within 14 days from the date of receipt of the goods.

Hair ornaments are non-returnable.

Return conditions:

We don’t accept returns for products containing cubic zircon and/or acrylic as precious metal wares, with precious stones, made of precious metals with inserts of semi-precious and synthetic stones, faceted with precious stones are included in the "List of non-food products of good quality that cannot be returned or exchanged" for a similar product of a different size, shape, dimension, style, color or configuration, approved by Decree of the Government of the dated January 19, 1998 No. 55
